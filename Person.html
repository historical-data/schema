Thing > Person

A historical or genealogical profile may include these additional attributes of Person:
Property Expected Type Description
Additional Properties for a historical Person
birth Event An event describing the details of a person's birth. (Replaces or enhances Person birthDate.)
burial Event An event describing the details of a person's burial.
christening Event An event describing the details of a person's baptism or christening.
death Event An event describing the details of a person's death. (Replaces or enhances Person deathDate)
event Event Event(s) describing a significant incident in the lifetime of a person. (Can be used in place of Person homeLocation, jobTitle, memberOf, worksFor and for other significant life events.)
gender Text Gender of the person. For a genealogical profile, recommended values are "male", "female", and "unknown".
marriage Event Event(s) describing the details of a person's marriage(s).
source HistoricalRecord The source(s) of the information on the person.
