Properties from Historical Record
contributor Person Person who uploaded or provided the record. See also Dublin Core: contributor.
content Text Text content of the record.
creator Historical Person or Organization. Person or organization (e.g. government agency, etc.) that created the record. See also Dublin Core: creator.
dates Date The date(s) and time periods that are referenced by the content of the record. This property is described by Dublin Core as "temporal coverage".
language Text The language of the content of the record. Please use of of the language codes from the IETF BCP 47 standard.
locations Date The place(s) and locations that are referenced by the content of the record. This property is described by Dublin Core as "spatial coverage".
persons HistoricalPerson Persons that are described or referenced by this record.
events HistoricalEvent Events that are described or referenced by this record.
families HistoricalFamily. Families that are described or referenced by this record.
note Text Note or comment related to the record.
modifiedDate Date Date of the most recent revision to the information about record.
type Text Type of record.
